# Explanify V2 — Product Vision

## Positioning
Explanify V2 is an AI-powered Project Coordination Platform that acts as the invisible 5th team member. It reduces coordination chaos for technical teams without replacing developers or their tools.

## Target Users
- Student technical teams
- Hackathon teams
- Startup developer teams

## Core Promise
- Understand project dependencies and workflow reality
- Detect blockers and predict delays early
- Balance workloads and guide recovery
- Explain risks and decisions transparently

## Principles
- **Tool-native**: work alongside VS Code, GitHub, and Figma via integrations
- **Explainable intelligence**: every recommendation includes reasoning, evidence, and confidence
- **Low-friction**: avoid process overhead; fit into existing team behavior
- **Scalable by design**: architecture supports growth from small teams to many orgs
- **MVP focus**: coordination intelligence over enterprise bloat

## Experience Goals
- Feels like a proactive teammate, not a manager
- Provides clarity during chaos without micromanaging
- Speaks in project terms: dependencies, critical paths, blockers, risk

## Success Metrics (MVP)
- Reduced time-to-unblock for critical tasks
- Increased on-time task completion for dependency-heavy projects
- Higher team awareness of risks and workloads
